What makes a good story?

Some things to consider:
  • Substance not headline?
  • something the audience can relate to?
  • topical?
  • achievable?
  • why now?
  • what’s the twist?
  • Balance?
  • Facts?

3 main
- What is the story?
- Who is going to tell it?
- How are you going to tell it?
