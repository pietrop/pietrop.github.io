It's good to be in the loop, having a heads up what's in development, and what's in production can help you clue up about which companies to research on and eventually apply to get involved in the next big thing.

There's a couple of website(unfortunately all a bit pricey)

BECTU - Earlybird production list

The listing is a benefit of membership and does not incur any additional cost. 8pcm?

Production intelligence - The Knowledge Online

The Knowledge Online - See What's in development/per-production/production
Subscription: £495 / year* Introductory period offer.


You can also register yourself to come up in the results, free of charge

 Know how 
Free section covering, Aviation, employment, filming on location, Health and safety, travel information. 
for instance there's an article on filming in London.

Broadcast Magazine

Broadcast Now
Subscription £19 per month. you receive the magazine every week and online acces.

Meet the commissioner is a very useful  page where in a glance you can see the commissioners categorised by broadcast and then by channel.
and you can also see interviews they do with them, this can help you tailor your showreel, and cv for the upcoming agenda if that's something you are interested in.

Broadcast Green-light offers the latest information on commissions and provides a comprehensive guide to indies, channels and commissioners.


" Subscriber only content
With a database of over 3,125 programmes, 325 commissioners and more than 500 indies, Greenlight saves you both research time and money. Get up-to-the-minute information on the latest commissions and a comprehensive guide to the indies, channels and the commissioners behind them."
Broadcast Jobs they also have their job board (free to sign up and get e-mail allerts)
