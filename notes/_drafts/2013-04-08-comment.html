3 useful translation tool useful for Research from Google


Google Translate Web

To translate web pages and still browse them with their original layout,
good for searching in a language you don't know


Google Translate Toolkit

To translate documents in another language, it helps generating a first draft of automatic translation keeping it organized, and side by side, for proofing it.



Google Translate

Most widely known, a side from the variety of languages, and at being good for quick translation it also has text to speech that is good to check pronunciation of foreign word in native language.
